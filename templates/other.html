Other

Plastic type 7, classified as "Other", encompasses a variety of plastics not included in the primary categories 1 through 6. This category often includes materials like polycarbonate and acrylic, which are used in items such as eyewear lenses, water bottles, and display cases. Because it covers a wide range of plastics with different properties, recycling facilities may face challenges in processing these materials. Consequently, not all recycling programs accept plastics labeled as type 7, so it's essential to check with local waste management services for appropriate disposal options. Proper identification and disposal are crucial to ensure these materials are handled correctly and do not contribute to environmental waste.
